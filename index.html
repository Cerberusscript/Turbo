Why Turbos?

The current blockchain landscape is riddled with issues such as high gas fees, slow transaction times, and scalability bottlenecks. These challenges hinder the growth and adoption of decentralized technologies in the mainstream. Turbos solves this problem by leveraging a high-speed, low-fee blockchain that ensures quick, secure transactions, enabling mass adoption of decentralized applications.
